Our Research & Reports

Discover Our Research Insights

Welcome to the Research & Report section, your go-to resource for the latest studies, insights, and data on child online protection.

Here, we provide comprehensive reports and research findings to help parents, educators, policymakers, and communities stay informed about the evolving landscape of digital safety for children.

Our Research Focus

1

Cybersecurity Threats

 In-depth analysis of current and emerging online threats targeting children, including cyberbullying, online predators, and privacy breaches.

2

Digital Literacy

Studies on the importance of digital literacy and how it empowers children to navigate the online world safely and responsibly.

3

Policy and Legislation

 Examination of global and regional policies, laws, and regulations designed to protect children online.

4

Technology Trends

Insights into how advancements in technology impact child online safety and what measures can be taken to mitigate risks.

Featured Reports

1

2023 Annual Child Online Safety Report

A comprehensive overview of the state of child online protection, including key statistics, trends, and recommendations for stakeholders.

2

Impact of Social Media on Youth
– A Global Perspective

An analysis of how social media platforms influence the behavior, mental health, and safety of young users.
3

Parental Controls and Their Effectiveness

A study on the various parental control tools available and their effectiveness in safeguarding children online.

Ongoing Research Projects

1

Digital Behavior Study

A longitudinal study tracking the online behavior of children and adolescents to identify patterns and potential risk factors.

2

Cyberbullying Prevention Strategies

Research focused on developing and testing effective strategies to prevent and address cyberbullying among youth.

3

Safe Tech Initiative

An evaluation of the safety features of popular apps and online platforms used by children, with recommendations for improvement.
